Welcome to Covid Stats EU

Are you lost about how Covid-19 affects each country in EU? Here you can view every activity by selecting an Action on the panel below and examining the Results that appear on the results table.

A

Display total number of cases, deaths and tests per EU country.

B

Show number of cases and deaths in a time period and country.

C

Display countries with cases above one threshold over a period of time.

D

Display the top-10 weeks with the highest test rates per population for each EU country.

View the top-10 weeks

E

Display the number of weeks that each EU country has been in the top-10 countries with the most cases.

View period in weeks

F

Display pairs of countries with very close numbers of cases or deaths within a time period given.

G

Insert new data for a country at specific week. (date will be converted to the corresponding week number)
